The Studio

We believe in the tangible weight of words. Situated in a quiet corner of the world, SHADE is a sanctuary for the printed medium.

Every book is bound by hand, every paper selected for its texture, every ink mixed for its depth. We merge traditional craftsmanship with modern minimalist aesthetics to create objects that demand to be held.
